  • Priority Learning: Tackle the most challenging topics first when your mind is fresh.
  • Peer Teaching: Explain concepts to a friend to deepen your own understanding.
  • Past Paper Practice: Use past papers to build exam technique and manage time effectively.
  • Spaced Revision: Review material after 1 day, then 1 week for better retention.
  • Active Note-Taking: Write in your own words, don't just copy.
